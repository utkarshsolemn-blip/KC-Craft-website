Our Story

Crafted with
Engineering.

K.C. Craft was established in 2006 in Mumbai by Sanjeev K. Jain (B.E. Mech), bringing an engineering mindset to fine jewellery manufacturing. Our mechanical expertise enables us to produce ultra-lightweight jewellery with exceptional precision and finish.

We specialize exclusively in fine jewellery chains and lightweight pieces — gold and silver — serving retailers, wholesalers, and exporters across India and internationally. Every piece reflects our commitment to engineering excellence and contemporary design.

As proud GJC Life Members and Platinium Members, and exhibitors at IIJS for the last 20 years, K.C. Craft is a name trusted by jewellery businesses worldwide.
